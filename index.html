Labors Category

Babysitter

Suitable to take care of babies from 0 to 2 years old. Starting from bathing and maintaining body hygiene, changing diapers, maintaining baby safety, baby activities, and much more.

Nanny

Once the baby gets older with a minimum age of 2 to 5 years, this is suitable for you. The duties and responsibilities of a nanny are the same as babysitters but imbued with the ability to educate and teach a child aged 2 - 5 years.

Elderly Caregiver

Caring for the elderly/sick includes serving physical needs (activities ranging from waking up to sleeping again), medical needs, physical therapy, social needs, and spiritual needs.

Cooking Assistance

Making and preparing meals according to the schedule and menu, and ensure hygiene and quality of the foods and beverages they serve.

Cleaning Assistance

Sweeping the inside of the house and the yard, mopping the floor, make rooms and beds, water and care for plants, cleaning the bathroom, washing dishes, and washing clothes.
